COLOPHON

NEABBS (2026) is a tribute to the Dutch bulletin-board culture
of the 1980s, and in particular to the systems that, with dozens
of lines at once, taught a whole generation that a computer can
be a place.

This system has no connection whatsoever with, and does not claim
to be the original of, any historical system whatsoever. The names,
handles and events on the boards are fiction. Any resemblance to
real persons rests on nostalgia.

In memory of Max Keizer (†2010), sysop from the very first hour.
Without him there would be nothing to return to.

Technology: one program, written in Go, reachable over SSH.
No shell, no filesystem, only this. Your key is your identity;
no passwords are stored because there are no passwords.

Post your memories on GENERAL. The sysop is reading.
The sysop is always reading.
