  NEABBS IS BACK

  Somewhere at the end of the '80s the last connection here
  dropped. Nobody hung up; the line just went quiet.
  For almost forty years it stayed dark.

  Max Keizer, who built NEABBS and for years kept it running as
  sysop, passed away in 2010. This re-release has been carefully
  assembled from surviving tapes — as a tribute to him, and to
  everyone who ever pulled an all-nighter here.

  Everything has been left as it was as much as possible. The
  boards, the files, the chatbox, the 24 lines.

  Welcome back. Or simply: welcome.

                                     -- the caretakers of the re-release
